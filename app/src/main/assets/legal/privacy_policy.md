# TrainPNR Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 13, 2026

## Overview
TrainPNR ("we", "our", "us") operates the TrainPNR mobile application. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect
- **Gameplay data:** Progress, scores, achievements, and preferences stored locally on your device.
- **Analytics data:** Anonymous usage statistics via Firebase Analytics (if consented).
- **Advertising data:** Ad interaction data via Google AdMob (if consented).

## How We Use Information
- Provide and improve game features
- Display personalized or non-personalized ads (based on consent)
- Analyze app performance and fix bugs

## Data Storage
Game progress is stored locally using Room database and DataStore. Sensitive preferences use EncryptedSharedPreferences.

## Third-Party Services
- Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config)
- Google AdMob (Advertising)

## Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)
You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting privacy@trainpnr.game.

## Children's Privacy
TrainPNR is not directed at children under 13.
